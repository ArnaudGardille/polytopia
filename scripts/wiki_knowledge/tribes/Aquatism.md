## Aquatism  
  
[ ![Aquatism Branch](images/Aquatism_Branch.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/5/54/Aquatism_Branch.png/revision/latest?cb=20240409001348> "Aquatism Branch")

### Tier

Three

### Preceding

[Ramming](</wiki/Ramming> "Ramming")

### Unlocks

[Water Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples"), defence bonus on water and ocean tiles

**Aquatism** is a tier three [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Water Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples") and grants a [defence bonus](</wiki/Defence_Bonus> "Defence Bonus") to friendly [units](<../general/Units.md> "Units") on water and ocean tiles. 

  

## Polarism[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FAquatism%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

[Polaris](<Polaris.md> "Polaris") has the **Polarism** technology instead of Aquatism. Polarism unlocks the [Ice Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples") and the Glide ability, which makes moving onto ice tiles cost half a move for all non-skating units, functionally similar to to how roads work. Moving from an ice tile to a onto a non-ice tile, even if it has a road, still costs one full [move](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement").
