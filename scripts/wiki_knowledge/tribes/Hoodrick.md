> The yellow autumn leaves of the Hoodrick woods are perfect hideouts for its peculiar mushroom stuffing inhabitants.
> 
> Tribe description

**Hoodrick** is the sixth [tribe](<../general/Tribes.md> "Tribe") in Polytopia. It is a regular tribe that starts with the [Archery](</wiki/Archery> "Archery") [technology](</wiki/Technology> "Technology") and an [Archer](</wiki/Archer> "Archer") [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit"). 

Hoodrick has immediate access to Archers, but this also means their starting unit is vulnerable to other tribes. Hoodrick player must be extra careful with where they choose to send their starting unit, lest they encounter an enemy early on and lose both their unit and the village. Economy wise however, the Hoodrick biome has the most forests out of any tribe, which not only can be used to build [Lumber Huts](</wiki/Lumber_Hut> "Lumber Hut") and [Sawmills](</wiki/Sawmill> "Sawmill") but also provide a defence bonus to Hoodrick units (since Hoodrick's starting technology, Archery, unlocks the defence bonus in forest). Hoodrick is thus best played conservatively early on, with their main power spike coming in the mid-to-late game when they can get their Sawmills online and get their Super Units.

## Inspiration[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHoodrick%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D5&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

The Hoodrick tribe is primarily inspired by Medieval English culture, but also encompasses other Western European cultures such as Germany, France and Spain. [3] Their half-timbered style of architecture was common across many areas of Western Europe, whilst the English mythological figure of Robin Hood provides inspiration for their appearance, name, and starting tech of archery.[4]

[![Hoodrick Appearance](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/6/66/Hoodrick_Appearance.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20161218191602>) [![Hoodrick Appearance](images/Hoodrick_Appearance.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/6/66/Hoodrick_Appearance.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20161218191602>) [](</wiki/File:Hoodrick_Appearance.JPG>)

  * Fruit: Quissberries (raspberries)
  * Animal: Hooxe (bull)
  * Field: Olive green-colored fields with pale soil
  * Forest: Maple trees with yellow- and orange-colored autumn leaves
  * Mountain: Hills considerably less tall than other tribes' mountains
  * City: Medieval English-style cottages and castle-like structures
  * Helmet: Green bycocket hat (common throughout medieval Western Europe) with red feather

[![YorthSplash](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/b/ba/YorthSplash.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20230401190203>) [![YorthSplash](images/YorthSplash.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/b/ba/YorthSplash.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20230401190203>) [](</wiki/File:YorthSplash.jpg>)

The splash screen for the Yorthwober skin in the Tribe Selection menu.

The Yorthwober skin was added in the first skin pack for Polytopia. Yorthwober outfits feature a green bandana over the mouth in addition to the default Hoodrick hat, a cape draped over the shoulders, a brown belt with a gold buckle, and darker footwear. 

  * The Archers have a unique composite recurve bow made of a darker wood with a red grip.
  * The Defenders have circular shields with bosses.
  * The city buildings have white bricks and red roofs.
