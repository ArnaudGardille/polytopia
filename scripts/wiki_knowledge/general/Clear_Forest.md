## Clear Forest  
  
[ ![](images/268.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/0/0a/Clear_forest.png/revision/latest?cb=20180603204447>) The Clear Forest icon

### Unlocked by

[Forestry](</wiki/Forestry> "Forestry")

### Effect

Turns a forest tile into a field tile and gives one star

**Clear Forest** is an ability that turns a forest tile into a field tile and gives the player one [star](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"). The ability is unlocked by the [Forestry](</wiki/Forestry> "Forestry") technology and can only be used on forest tiles in the player's territory without any [buildings or structures](</wiki/Buildings> "Buildings") on them. If the ability is used on a tile with a wild animal, the wild animal will be forfeited. 

The [∑∫ỹriȱŋ](<∑∫ỹriȱŋ.md> "∑∫ỹriȱŋ") tribe does not have access to this ability. 

## History[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FClear_Forest%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

In [update 2.0.49](<Update_History.md> "Update History"), Clear Forest was moved from the [Forestry](</wiki/Forestry> "Forestry") technology to Free Spirit, and the number of stars each forest produced when cleared was reduced from two to one. In update 2.0.58, the star reward was reverted to two stars. In update 2.1, Clear Forest was once again reduced to one star but moved back to Forestry.
