The **Scout** is a naval [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") that is an upgrade from the [Raft](<Raft.md> "Raft"). The health of a Scout is determined by the unit it carries. 

A Raft can be upgraded into a Scout for five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"), once [Sailing](</wiki/Sailing> "Sailing") is researched. Upgrading a naval unit does not heal it. 

Moving an upgraded naval unit onto land causes the unit to permanently lose its upgrade: if that unit is once again moved onto a [Port](<Port.md> "Port"), it will become a Raft. It should be noted that when a Scout moves onto land it will still reveal fog in a 5x5 radius before it disembarks. 

The Scout replaced the scouting aspect of the [Battleship](</wiki/Battleship> "Battleship") for the [Path of the Ocean](</wiki/Path_of_the_Ocean> "Path of the Ocean") update. While the Scout has the scouting aspect of the [Battleship](</wiki/Battleship> "Battleship"), it is a lot closer to the [Ship](</wiki/Ship> "Ship") in appearance and usage, having the same Attack, Range, and [Movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") as the Ship. It is used for both offence and exploration.
