### Tribe

[Zebasi](<../tribes/Zebasi.md> "Zebasi"), [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti") (modified)

**Farming** is a tier two [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Farm](</wiki/Farm> "Farm"), a [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") that costs five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"), provides two [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population"), and can only be built on tiles with crop. 

For the [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe"), Farming unlocks the [Fungi](</wiki/Fungi> "Fungi"), a building whose population produced grows over time, instead of the Farm. 

[Zebasi](<../tribes/Zebasi.md> "Zebasi") and [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti") start with Farming.
