# Unit Skills

**Source:** https://polytopia.fandom.com/wiki/Skills  
**Licence:** CC-BY-SA  
**Date de scraping:** 2025-11-24

**Unit skills** , also called **unit abilities** , give [units](<Units.md> "Units") special characteristics in [combat](<../game_mechanics/Combat.md> "Combat") or [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement"). A unit may have zero or multiple skills.

**Grow** | Allows a unit to grow into a different unit after a given number of turns.  |

  * [Mind Bender](</wiki/Mind_Bender> "Mind Bender")

  
**Hide** | Allows a unit to hide itself and become invisible to enemies when it moves.  | 

  * [Cloak](</wiki/Cloak> "Cloak")
  * [Dinghy](</wiki/Dinghy> "Dinghy")

  
**Independent** | Units with this skill do not take up a [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population") slot in or belong to any city.  |

**Persist** | Allows a unit to attack again immediately after killing an enemy unit. There is no limit on the number of kills in a single turn.  |
