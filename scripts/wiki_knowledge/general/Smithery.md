## Smithery  
  
[ ![Smithery Branch](images/Smithery_Branch.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/f/fe/Smithery_Branch.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408231217> "Smithery Branch")

### Tier

Three

### Preceding

[Mining](</wiki/Mining> "Mining")

### Tribe

[Vengir](<../tribes/Vengir.md> "Vengir")

**Smithery** is a tier three [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Swordsman](</wiki/Swordsman> "Swordsman"), a powerful front-line melee [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit"), and the [Forge](</wiki/Forge> "Forge"), a [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") that provides two [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population") for each adjacent [Mine](<Mine.md> "Mine"). 

The [Vengir](<../tribes/Vengir.md> "Vengir") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe") starts with Smithery. Starting with Smithery does not allow researching [Mining](</wiki/Mining> "Mining"), as technologies cannot be researched backwards.
