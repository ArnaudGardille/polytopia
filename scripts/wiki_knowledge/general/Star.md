## Star

[ ![Star](images/Star.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/2/2e/Star.png/revision/latest?cb=20211229172010> "Star")

The **star** is the in-game currency of Polytopia. It is used for training [units](</wiki/Units> "Units"), buying [technologies](</wiki/Technology> "Technology"), harvesting resources and constructing [buildings](</wiki/Buildings> "Buildings"). 

Stars are produced every turn by [cities](</wiki/Cities> "Cities") and buildings like the [Market](</wiki/Market> "Market"). This income is measured in stars per turn (SPT) and is shown in brackets next to the current amount of stars. One-time sources of stars can also be obtained from various methods, outlined below. 

[![A 2016 screenshot in which the in-game currency is called "resources](images/A_2016_screenshot_in_which_the_in-game_currency_is_called__resources.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/3/38/Hoodrick_world.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20160422144633>) [](</wiki/File:Hoodrick_world.PNG>)

A 2016 screenshot in which the in-game currency is called "resources."

Stars were originally called "**resources**." They still represent resources in the Polytopian world.[1]

## Income Sources[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FStar%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

Item  | Income (stars per turn)   
---|---  
[Cities](</wiki/Cities> "Cities") | 1 per level[note 1]  
[Clathrus](</wiki/Clathrus> "Clathrus") | 1 per adjacent [Algae](</wiki/Algae> "Algae")  
[Ice Bank](</wiki/Ice_Bank> "Ice Bank") | 3 per every 20 frozen tiles in the world; max. 30 SPT   
[Market](</wiki/Market> "Market") | 2 per each level of adjacent [Windmill](</wiki/Windmill> "Windmill"), [Sawmill](</wiki/Sawmill> "Sawmill"), or [Forge](</wiki/Forge> "Forge"), max. level 8   
[Sanctuary](</wiki/Sanctuary> "Sanctuary") | 1 per adjacent wild animal   
  
  1. ↑ The income each city produces is shown to the right of the city's name. The [Workshop](</wiki/Workshop> "Workshop") and [Park](</wiki/Park> "Park") increase a city’s income by 1 SPT. Capital cities may produce more income; see [Cities](</wiki/Cities#Capitals> "Cities") for details. Cities under siege do not produce any income.
