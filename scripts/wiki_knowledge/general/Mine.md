## Mine  
  
[ ![Mine](images/Mine.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/a/a0/Mine.png/revision/latest?cb=20180614122255> "Mine")

### Technology

[Mining](</wiki/Mining> "Mining")

### Cost

5 stars

### Effect

Produces two population

The **Mine** is a [building](<Buildings.md> "Buildings") that costs five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars") and produces two [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population"). The building is unlocked by the [Mining](</wiki/Mining> "Mining") [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") and can only be built on mountains with metal. (Metal is only visible after [Climbing](<../technology/Climbing.md> "Climbing") has been researched.)
