## Free Spirit  
  
[ ![Chivalry Branch](images/Chivalry_Branch.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/5/59/Chivalry_Branch.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408230918> "Chivalry Branch")

### Tier

Two

### Preceding

[Riding](</wiki/Riding> "Riding")

### Following

[Chivalry](</wiki/Chivalry> "Chivalry")

### Unlocks

[Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples"), [Disband](<Disband.md> "Disband")

  
**Free Spirit** is a tier two [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples"), an expensive [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") whose [point value](<../game_mechanics/Score.md> "Score") increases over time, and the the [Disband](<Disband.md> "Disband") ability, which removes a [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") and returns half of its cost, rounded down.

[Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion") has the **Free Diving** technology instead of Free Spirit. Free Diving unlocks [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") in ocean as well as the Temple and Disband.
