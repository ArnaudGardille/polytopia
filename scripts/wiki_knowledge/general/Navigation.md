## Navigation  
  
[ ![Navigation](images/Navigation.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/4/4f/Navigation.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408233214> "Navigation")

### Tier

Three

### Preceding

[Sailing](</wiki/Sailing> "Sailing")

### Unlocks

[Bomber](</wiki/Bomber> "Bomber"), [Starfish Harvesting](<Starfish_Harvesting.md> "Starfish Harvesting")

**Navigation** is a tier three [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Bomber](</wiki/Bomber> "Bomber") [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") and the ability to harvest [Starfish](</wiki/Starfish> "Starfish"). 

Navigation is a crucial technology on maps that require long-distance naval bombardments. 

## Polar Warfare[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FNavigation%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

[Polaris](<../tribes/Polaris.md> "Polaris") has the **Polar Warfare** technology instead of Navigation. Polar Warfare unlocks the [Ice Fortress](</wiki/Ice_Fortress> "Ice Fortress") and the ability to harvest Starfish. 

## Oceanology[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FNavigation%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

[Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti") has the **Oceanology** technology instead of Navigation. Oceanology unlocks the ability to harvest starfish. 

## **Before Path of the Ocean**[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FNavigation%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

Before the Path of the Ocean update, Navigation unlocked the [Battleship](</wiki/Battleship> "Battleship") [unit](<Units.md> "Units"), [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") on ocean tiles, and the [Explorer task](</wiki/Eye_of_God> "Eye of God").
