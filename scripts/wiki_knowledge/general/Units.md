# Units

**Units** are troops used for expansion and [combat](<../game_mechanics/Combat.md> "Combat").   
  
There are 42 unit types in _The Battle of Polytopia_. Every type has unique stats and [skills](</wiki/Unit_Skills> "Unit Skills"). The most basic unit type is the [Warrior](</wiki/Warrior> "Warrior"). Other types include the [Rider](</wiki/Rider> "Rider"), which has extra movement, and the [Archer](</wiki/Archer> "Archer"), which has a ranged attack. Every unit type is unlocked by a [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology"), except for the Warrior. Some unit types are unique to a special [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe"). Every unit type has an unique icon shown on the map at the unit's upper right. For example, the icon for a Rider is shaped like the head of a horse. These icons can be used to determine at a glance which unit a naval vessel carries. The white outline of a unit's icon disappears when it cannot make any attacks, which is useful for identifying if a unit with the _escape_ skill can attack after moving. 

Units are primarily obtained through training in [cities](</wiki/Cities> "Cities"). The cost to train a unit depends on its type. Inversely, a player can [disband](</wiki/Disband> "Disband") a unit, which removes the unit and returns half of its cost (rounded down). 

A [super unit](</wiki/Super_Unit> "Super Unit") is the most powerful unit of its tribe. They are primarily obtained through [city upgrades](</wiki/Cities#City_Upgrades> "Cities"). The super unit for all regular tribes is the [Giant](</wiki/Giant> "Giant"). Special tribes have unique super units. 

The aesthetics of each individual unit depends on the tribe that it belongs to. Every tribe has a unique color and helmet. (In mirror matches where multiple players pick the same tribe, different players of the same tribe are distinguished by color.) Also, the animals that mounted units (like the [Rider](</wiki/Rider> "Rider")) ride on vary from tribe to tribe. Finally, the [Battleship](</wiki/Battleship> "Battleship") had a tribe-specific decoration until it was removed.

## Unit Stats[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FUnits%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

[![Warrior Info](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/e/e4/Warrior_Info.png/revision/latest?cb=20201215202102>) [![Warrior Info](images/Warrior_Info.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/e/e4/Warrior_Info.png/revision/latest?cb=20201215202102>) [](</wiki/File:Warrior_Info.png>)

The information box of a Warrior, which includes its stats.

The stats of each individual unit are associated with its type. Every unit type has unique stats. 

Stat  | Description   
---|---  
Health  | The maximum health of the unit. The health stat of veteran units is increased by 5 HP.   
Attack  | Determines how much damage the unit deals when it attacks. For more information, see [Combat](<../game_mechanics/Combat.md> "Combat").   
Defence  | Determines how much retaliation damage the unit deals and how much damage it takes when attacked. For more information, see [Combat](<../game_mechanics/Combat.md> "Combat").   
Movement  | Determines how far the unit can move. For more information, see [Movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement").   
Range  | Determines how close enemy units have to be for the unit to attack them. A unit can attack enemy units whose [chessboard distance](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chebyshev_distance> "wikipedia:Chebyshev distance") is not more than the unit's range.   
  
## Veteran Units[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FUnits%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D5&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

A unit can be promoted to a veteran after it kills three enemy units (through attacks or retaliation). Promoting a unit increases its maximum health by five and then heals the unit completely. Therefore, a player may choose to not immediately promote a unit when it becomes eligible to do so because the promotion can be used as a "free" heal later. 

Naval units, super units, the [Dagger](</wiki/Dagger> "Dagger") and [Pirate](</wiki/Pirate> "Pirate"), enchanted units (the [Polytaur](</wiki/Polytaur> "Polytaur") and the [Navalon](</wiki/Navalon> "Navalon")), and units that are not controlled by any player (the [Bunny](</wiki/Bunny> "Bunny") and the [Bunta](</wiki/Bunta> "Bunta")) cannot be promoted.
