## Hunting  
  
[ ![Hunting](images/Hunting.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/3/39/Hunting.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408230057> "Hunting")

### Tier

One

### Following

[Archery](<../units/Archery.md> "Archery"), [Forestry](</wiki/Forestry> "Forestry")

### Unlocks

Hunting of wild animals

### Tribe

[Bardur](<../tribes/Bardur.md> "Bardur"), [∑∫ỹriȱŋ](<∑∫ỹriȱŋ.md> "∑∫ỹriȱŋ") (as Forest Magic)

**Hunting** is a tier one [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the hunting of wild animals (a.k.a. game). Hunting costs two [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars") and produces one [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population"). 

The [Bardur](<../tribes/Bardur.md> "Bardur") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe") starts with Hunting. 

Hunting's branches in the technology tree are focused on forest management.[1]
