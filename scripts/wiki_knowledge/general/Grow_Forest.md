## Grow Forest  
  
[ ![GrowForest](images/GrowForest.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/5/5d/GrowForest.png/revision/latest?cb=20200603224245> "GrowForest")

### Unlocked by

[Spiritualism](</wiki/Spiritualism> "Spiritualism")

### Cost

5 stars

### Effect

Turns a field tile into a forest tile.

**Grow Forest** is an ability that turns a field tile into a forest tile at a cost of five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"). The ability is unlocked by the [Spiritualism](</wiki/Spiritualism> "Spiritualism") [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") and can only be used on field tiles in the player's territory without any [buildings](</wiki/Buildings> "Buildings") on them. 

The Grow Forest ability can be used to build a [Forest Temple](</wiki/Temples> "Temples") on a field tile. Also, it can be used with the [Burn Forest](<Burn_Forest.md> "Burn Forest") ability and [Farming](</wiki/Farming> "Farming") to produce [population](</wiki/Population> "Population") from an empty field tile by growing then burning a forest and finally building a [Farm](</wiki/Farm> "Farm"). In addition, Grow Forest has a limited military use; because forests hinder [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement"), they can be used to frustrate invading enemies.
