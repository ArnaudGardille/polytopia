# Buildings

**Source:** https://polytopia.fandom.com/wiki/Monuments  
**Licence:** CC-BY-SA  
**Date de scraping:** 2025-11-24

---

A **building** is an immovable object located on a tile. There are three main categories of buildings: resource buildings, monuments, and [temples](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples"). Other buildings include [bridges](<../buildings/Bridge.md> "Bridge"), [roads](<../buildings/Roads_\(Building\).md> "Roads \(Building\)"), and the [Embassy](<../buildings/Embassy.md> "Embassy"). Buildings are constructed by the player. Each type of building can only be built on specific [terrain](<../game_mechanics/Terrain.md> "Terrain") types.
