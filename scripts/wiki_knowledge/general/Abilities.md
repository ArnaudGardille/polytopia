**Abilities** are actions that can be performed on a tile. Each ability can only be used on a tile with a certain [terrain](<../game_mechanics/Terrain.md> "Terrain"), resource, or [building](</wiki/Building> "Building"). Abilities include [Burn Forest](</wiki/Burn_Forest> "Burn Forest"), [Clear Forest](</wiki/Clear_Forest> "Clear Forest"), [Destroy](</wiki/Destroy> "Destroy"), [Grow Forest](</wiki/Grow_Forest> "Grow Forest"), and [Whale Hunting](</wiki/Whale_Hunting> "Whale Hunting").
