**Yădakk** the 12th and final regular [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe") in Polytopia. It starts with the [Roads](</wiki/Roads_\(Technology\)> "Roads \(Technology\)") [technology](</wiki/Technology> "Technology"). 

Yădakk is an upper-mid-tier tribe with decent resources and a useful starting technology.

## Inspiration[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FY%25C4%2583dakk%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D5&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

The design of the Yădakk is inspired by the cultures of peoples living along the Silk Road, including the Turkish and Mongolian cultures.[3] This theme was inspired by the Mongol-themed fan-made tribe [Moriton](<https://polytopia-fanmade-tribes-rewritten.fandom.com/wiki/Moriton> "w:c:polytopia-fanmade-tribes-rewritten:Moriton").[4]

The Khamelk (Yădakk animal) was inspired by [Kamälgen](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/sv:Kam%C3%A4lgen> "wp:sv:Kamälgen"), a monument in [Midjiwan](</wiki/Midjiwan> "Midjiwan")'s hometown that is a cross between the camel and the moose. The name of the Khamelk is a portmanteau of the words "camel" and "elk."

### Ürkaz Skin[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FY%25C4%2583dakk%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D12&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

> The gruff Ürkaz from the Khalee Highlands control the criminal underground of the Yădakk, and nothing is off limits are they establish economic domination of the Square!
> 
> Ürkaz skin description

[![Urkazsplash](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/7/70/Urkazsplash.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20240204041157>) [![Urkazsplash](images/Urkazsplash.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/7/70/Urkazsplash.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20240204041157>) [](</wiki/File:Urkazsplash.jpg>)

The splash screen for the Ürkaz skin in the Tribe Selection menu.

The Ürkaz skin was added in the fourth skin pack for Polytopia. They wear a furred cap instead of the typical Yădakk fez, and pouches at their sides. 

  * The fruit are replaced with limes known as Lizuz.
  * The animals are replaced with a yak known as an Orda Beast.
  * The city buildings incorporate animal hides, banners, and food containers.
  * The Riders' saddles have tassels.
  * The Knights' shields and lances appear more tattered.
  * The Defenders have round shields with patches.
  * The Giants' shoes have brown soles.

  * Yădakk's [extended theme](</wiki/Polytopia_Music> "Polytopia Music"), _Saladik Ătjum_ , is the only one to have a [music video](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ztYiY8gQxp0>). The music video showcases Midjiwan and Midjitone (the developer and sound designer of Polytopia, respectively) acting as the "Yădakk Orkestar" and playing several different instruments.[5]
  * Yădakk is the first tribe[6] to have a fan-made Tribe Celebration poster, with its Tribe Moon poster being created by Discord user poopmaster6000.[7]
  * The Ürkaz skin's animal, the Orda Beast, is named after the Turkish word for "over there." This is an intentional pun on how it "gets [its riders] there," intended as a bonus for bilingual players.[8]
  * There exist alternate versions of the Ürkaz tribe head, city buildings, and animal that are slightly different from those used in-game. 
    * The alternate Ürkaz animal is the only sprite in the game that is not viewed from an isometric perspective. This is for aesthetic reasons.
  * The "leader" of the Yădakk tribe is named "Azurkaz".
