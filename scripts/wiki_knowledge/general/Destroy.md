## Destroy  
  
[ ![](images/268.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/0/0d/Destroy.png/revision/latest?cb=20190226153818>) The Destroy icon

### Unlocked by

[Chivalry](</wiki/Chivalry> "Chivalry")

### Cost

None

### Effect

Destroys a building or Ruin

**Destroy** is an ability that removes a [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") or [ruin](</wiki/Ruins> "Ruins") at no cost. The ability is unlocked by the [Chivalry](</wiki/Chivalry> "Chivalry") [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology"). 

Destroying a building also removes any [population](</wiki/Population> "Population") or [points](</wiki/Points> "Points") provided by the building. Destroyed monuments and [ice banks](</wiki/Ice_Bank> "Ice Bank") cannot be rebuilt. 

Destroy can be used in "scorched-earth" tactics before a city is captured by an enemy. 

## Decompose[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FDestroy%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

The [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe") has the **Decompose** ability instead of Destroy. The two abilities are identical except that the Decompose ability removes the building or ruin at the end of the turn (instead of immediately) and returns a building's full cost.
