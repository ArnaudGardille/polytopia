## Ice Bank  
  
[ ![](images/268.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/3/3b/Ice_bank_level_2.png/revision/latest?cb=20200428042117>) A level 2 Ice Bank

### Tribe

[Polaris](<../tribes/Polaris.md> "Polaris")

### Technology

[Trade](</wiki/Trade> "Trade")

### Cost

20 stars

### Effect

+2 income for every 20 frozen tiles.

The **Ice Bank** is a [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") unique to the [Polaris](<../tribes/Polaris.md> "Polaris") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribe") that costs 20 [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars") and produces 2 stars per turn for every 20 frozen land or ice tiles in the world (regardless of which Polaris player froze them). The Ice Bank's maximum level is level 30. Each Polaris player can only have one Ice Bank, which can be built on field or ice tiles. 

The Ice Bank is unlocked with the [Trade](</wiki/Trade> "Trade") [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") and replaces the [Market](</wiki/Market> "Market") for Polaris. 

## History[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FIce_Bank%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

In [update 1.14.1](<Update_History.md> "Update History"), the maximum level of the Ice Bank was increased from 8 to 10. This may have been to offset how the lack of the [Customs House](</wiki/Customs_House> "Customs House") made Polaris weak in [Perfection](</wiki/Perfection> "Perfection") before the advent of mirror matches in [Moonrise](<The_Battle_of_Polytopia__Moonrise.md> "Moonrise"), which allowed the Ice Bank to benefit from tiles frozen by other Polaris players. 

Later, the maximum level of the Ice Bank was again increased from 10 to its current maximum of 30, presumably in order to compete against the Customs House's economic value in the late game. 

In the [Path of the Ocean](</wiki/Path_of_the_Ocean> "Path of the Ocean") update, the amount of stars per level of the Ice Bank was reduced from 3 to 2, reducing the maximum number of stars that an Ice Bank can produce from 90 to 60.
