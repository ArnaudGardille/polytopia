The technology tree of a [Xin-xi](<../tribes/Xin-xi.md> "Xin-xi") player with all technology researched.

**Technology** is research that unlocks new resources, [buildings](</wiki/Buildings> "Buildings"), [units](</wiki/Units> "Units"), and/or [abilities](</wiki/Category:Abilities> "Category:Abilities"). Technology can be researched using [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars") and discovered in [ruins](</wiki/Ruins> "Ruins"). Each [tribe](</wiki/Tribes> "Tribes"), except for [Luxidoor](<../tribes/Luxidoor.md> "Luxidoor") and [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion"), has a unique starting technology that is unlocked at the start of the game. 

Technology is divided into three tiers. Tier one (T1) technology can immediately be researched at the start of the game, while tier two (T2) and tier three (T3) technology can be only researched after the previous technology in their branch is unlocked. 

The cost of a technology depends on its tier and the number of [cities](</wiki/Cities> "Cities") controlled by the player. Initially, tier one technology cost five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"), tier two cost six, and tier three cost seven. For each additional city the player controls, research costs increase by one, two, or three stars for T1, T2, and T3 technology, respectively. In other words, the cost of a technology is calculated as follows: `(technology tier) × (number of cities) + 4`. 

[![The Literacy icon](images/The_Literacy_icon.webp)](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/6/63/Literacy.png/revision/latest?cb=20180606210341>) [](</wiki/File:Literacy.png>)

The Literacy icon

Literacy, an ability unlocked by the [Philosophy](</wiki/Philosophy> "Philosophy") technology, reduces research costs by 33%. (Discounted costs are rounded up to the nearest star.)
