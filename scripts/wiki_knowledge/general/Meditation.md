## Meditation  
  
[ ![Philosophy](images/Philosophy.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/c/c5/Philosophy.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20240408231045> "Philosophy")

### Tier

Two

### Preceding

[Climbing](<../technology/Climbing.md> "Climbing")

### Following

[Philosophy](</wiki/Philosophy> "Philosophy")

### Unlocks

[Mountain Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples"), [Pacifist task](</wiki/Altar_of_Peace> "Altar of Peace")

### Tribe

[Ai-Mo](<../tribes/Ai-Mo.md> "Ai-Mo")

**Meditation** is a tier two [technology](<Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Mountain Temple](<../buildings/Temples.md> "Temples") and the Pacifist task, which grants the [Altar of Peace](</wiki/Altar_of_Peace> "Altar of Peace") if no attacks are made for five consecutive turns. 

The [Ai-Mo](<../tribes/Ai-Mo.md> "Ai-Mo") [tribe](<Tribes.md> "Tribes") starts with Meditation.

In [update 2.0.58](<Update_History.md> "Update History"), the [defence bonus](</wiki/Defence_Bonus> "Defence Bonus") in mountain tiles was moved from Meditation to [Climbing](<../technology/Climbing.md> "Climbing").
