## Farm  
  
[ ![Farm](images/Farm.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/a/a7/Farm.png/revision/latest?cb=20190301143906> "Farm")

### Technology

[Farming](<Farming.md> "Farming")

### Cost

5 stars

### Effect

Produces two population.

    _This article is about the land building. For the building unique to Aquarion, see[Aqua Farm](</wiki/Aqua_Farm> "Aqua Farm")._

The **Farm** is a [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") that costs five [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars") and produces two [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population"). The building is unlocked by the [Farming](<Farming.md> "Farming") technology and can only be built on fields with crop (which is visible after [Organization](<../technology/Organization.md> "Organization") or Farming has been researched). 

The [Windmill](</wiki/Windmill> "Windmill") produces one population per adjacent Farm.

The Advanced Farm

There is an unused sprite for an "Advanced Farm" that looks like a Farm covered with a clear dome. This may have been intended as an upgrade to the Farm.
