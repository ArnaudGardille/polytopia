    _This article is about types of tiles. For information on map generation and spawn rates, see[Map Generation](<Map_Generation.md> "Map Generation")._  
  
Every tile on the map belongs to one of several **terrain** types. The primary terrain types are field, forest, mountain, water, and ocean. A tile's terrain type determines the types of resources that can spawn and the types of [buildings](</wiki/Buildings> "Buildings") that can be built on it. In addition, certain terrain types reduce [movement](</wiki/Movement> "Movement"). The movement penalty may be removed by unlocking the relevant [technology](</wiki/Technology> "Technology") or may be permanent for some or all [units](</wiki/Units> "Units").
