## Archery

[ ![Spiritualism](images/Spiritualism.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/b/b0/Spiritualism.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408230428> "Spiritualism")

### Tier

Two

### Preceding

[Hunting](</wiki/Hunting> "Hunting")

### Following

[Spiritualism](</wiki/Spiritualism> "Spiritualism")

### Unlocks

[Archer](</wiki/Archer> "Archer"), defence bonus on forest tiles

### Tribe

[Hoodrick](<../tribes/Hoodrick.md> "Hoodrick")

**Archery** is a tier two [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Archer](</wiki/Archer> "Archer") [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") and a [defence bonus](</wiki/Defence_Bonus> "Defence Bonus") to friendly units on forest tiles. 

The [Hoodrick](<../tribes/Hoodrick.md> "Hoodrick") [tribe](<../general/Tribes.md> "Tribes") starts with Archery. 

For [Polaris](<../tribes/Polaris.md> "Polaris"), Archery unlocks the [Ice Archer](</wiki/Ice_Archer> "Ice Archer"),that freezes enemies instead of doing damage to them. For [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti"), Archery unlocks the [Phychi](</wiki/Phychi> "Phychi"), a special unit with the ability to travel over water and poison enemy units but a low attack and health, and the defence bonus in forest.
