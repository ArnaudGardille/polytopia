### Skills [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/b/b2/Info_Icon.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20220530151508)](<../general/Unit_Skills.md> "Unit Skills")[![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](<../general/Unit_Skills.md> "Unit Skills")

Dash, Fortify

The **Warrior** is a cheap, primitive melee [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") unlocked at the beginning of the game. Most [tribes](<../general/Tribes.md> "Tribes") start the game with a Warrior. 

The Warrior is a general-purpose unit great for exploration and front-line [combat](<../game_mechanics/Combat.md> "Combat") in the early- to mid-game. However, it is outclassed by the [Swordsman](</wiki/Swordsman> "Swordsman"), an all-around upgrade of the Warrior, and it is easily killed by stronger units like the [Catapult](</wiki/Catapult> "Catapult") and the [Knight](</wiki/Knight> "Knight").

Dash, Fortify, Amphibious

    _This article is about the mermaid version of the Warrior. For mermaid units in general, see[Mermaid Units](</wiki/Mermaid_Units> "Mermaid Units")._

The [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion") tribe has the **Mermaid** unit instead of the Warrior. Like other [mermaid units](</wiki/Mermaid_units> "Mermaid units"), the Mermaid differs from the Warrior only in being amphibious, allowing the Mermaid to move through both water and land, with a movement penalty on the latter.

## Trivia[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FWarrior%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

  * Costing only 2 stars, the Warrior is tied with the [Mermaid](</wiki/Mermaid> "Mermaid") as the cheapest unit in the entire game.
  * The Warrior is the only unit that does not require any [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") to unlock.
  * Prior to the addition of the Mermaid in the Aquarion rework, the Warrior was one of the only units that can be trained by all tribes. The others included the Swordsman and the [Cloak](</wiki/Cloak> "Cloak").
  * Of the 16 tribes in the game, seven of them do not start with a Warrior. They are: 
    * [Oumaji](<../tribes/Oumaji.md> "Oumaji"), which starts with a [Rider](</wiki/Rider> "Rider").
    * [Hoodrick](<../tribes/Hoodrick.md> "Hoodrick"), which starts with an [Archer](</wiki/Archer> "Archer").
    * [Vengir](<../tribes/Vengir.md> "Vengir"), which starts with a Swordsman.
    * [Quetzali](<../tribes/Quetzali.md> "Quetzali"), which starts with a [Defender](</wiki/Defender> "Defender").
    * [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion"), which starts with an [Amphibian](</wiki/Amphibian> "Amphibian").
    * [Polaris](<../tribes/Polaris.md> "Polaris"), which starts with a [Mooni](</wiki/Mooni> "Mooni").
    * [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti"), which starts with a [Shaman](</wiki/Shaman> "Shaman").
    * [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion"), which starts with a [Mermaid](</wiki/Mermaid> "Mermaid").
