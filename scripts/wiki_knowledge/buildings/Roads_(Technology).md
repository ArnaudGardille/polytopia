The road icon.

**Roads** is a tier two [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology"). It unlocks the [building](</wiki/Building> "Building") [of the same name](<Roads_\(Building\).md> "Roads \(Building\)"), which increases [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") (see [Movement#Roads](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") for more details) and [connects cities](</wiki/City_Connections> "City Connections"). This technology also unlocks the [bridge](<Bridge.md> "Bridge"), which functions the same, but on water. Lastly, it also unlocks the Network task, which awards the [Grand Bazaar](</wiki/Grand_Bazaar> "Grand Bazaar") once five cities are connected to the capital. 

The [Yădakk](<../general/Yădakk.md> "Yădakk") [tribe](<../general/Tribes.md> "Tribe") starts with Roads. 

For [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti"), Roads unlocks the [Mycelium](</wiki/Mycelium> "Mycelium") and the Network task. 

## Waterways[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FRoads_%28Technology%29%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

The [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion") tribe has the **Waterways** technology instead of Roads. Waterways unlocks the [Atoll](<../general/Atoll.md> "Atoll") and grants the Bubbled ability, that gives units a speed bubble whenever they move onto flooded tiles which increases their movement by 1 until they are attacked or move onto non-flooded land.
