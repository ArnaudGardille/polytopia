### Tribe

[Oumaji](<../tribes/Oumaji.md> "Oumaji"), [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion")

  
**Riding** is a tier one [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks the [Rider](</wiki/Rider> "Rider"), a [unit](</wiki/Unit> "Unit") with more movement but less defence than the [Warrior](<../units/Warrior.md> "Warrior"). The [Oumaji](<../tribes/Oumaji.md> "Oumaji") and [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion") tribes start with Riding. 

For [Aquarion](<../tribes/Aquarion.md> "Aquarion"), Riding unlocks the [Amphibian](</wiki/Amphibian> "Amphibian"), a special unit that replaces the Rider, and movement on shallow water. 

For [Cymanti](<../tribes/Cymanti.md> "Cymanti"), Riding unlocks the [Hexapod](</wiki/Hexapod> "Hexapod"), an agile, high-attack, but low-health special unit that replaces the Rider. 

Riding's branches in the technology tree are focused on "destructive aspects."[1]
