## Organization  
  
[ ![Organization Branches](images/Organization_Branches.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/4/46/Organization_Branches.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408230147> "Organization Branches")

### Tier

One

### Following

[Farming](</wiki/Farming> "Farming"), [Strategy](</wiki/Strategy> "Strategy")

### Unlocks

Harvest Fruit, reveals crop

### Tribe

[Imperius](<../tribes/Imperius.md> "Imperius")

**Organization** is a tier one [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks Harvest Fruit, which costs two [stars](</wiki/Stars> "Stars"), produces one [population](<../city/Population.md> "Population"), and can only be performed on tiles with fruit. Organization also reveals crop, a resource used by the [Farm](</wiki/Farm> "Farm"). 

The [Imperius](<../tribes/Imperius.md> "Imperius") tribe starts with Organization.
