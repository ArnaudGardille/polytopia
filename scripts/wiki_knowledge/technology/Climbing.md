## Climbing

[ ![Climbing Branches](images/Climbing_Branches.webp) ](<https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/supertribes/images/f/f9/Climbing_Branches.png/revision/latest?cb=20240408225655> "Climbing Branches")

### Tier

One

### Following

[Meditation](</wiki/Meditation> "Meditation"), [Mining](</wiki/Mining> "Mining")

### Unlocks

Defence bonus and movement in mountain tiles, reveals metal

### Tribe

[Xin-xi](<../tribes/Xin-xi.md> "Xin-xi")

**Climbing** is a tier one [technology](<../general/Technology.md> "Technology") that unlocks [movement](<../game_mechanics/Movement.md> "Movement") and a [defence bonus](</wiki/Defence_bonus> "Defence bonus") in mountain tiles and reveals metal, a resource used by the [Mine](</wiki/Mine> "Mine"). 

The [Xin-xi](<../tribes/Xin-xi.md> "Xin-xi") [tribe](<../general/Tribes.md> "Tribe") starts with Climbing. 

## History[[](<https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fpolytopia.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FClimbing%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en> "Sign in to edit")]

In [update 2.0.57](<../general/Update_History.md> "Update History"), the defence bonus in mountain tiles was moved from [Meditation](</wiki/Meditation> "Meditation") to Climbing.
